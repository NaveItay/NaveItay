About

My name is Itay

Hello! I am an Embedded/Firmware Software Engineer with a focus on C, C++, and Python. Bringing over 5 years of experience to the table, I offer comprehensive expertise throughout the entire product development lifecycle, from the initial concept to mass production.

In leading embedded development projects, my strengths in code architecture and design become particularly evident. This is complemented by my adept management of complex logic and intricate state machines. Additionally, I have developed a specialization in RF systems, with a focus on crafting energy-efficient and low-power solutions. This holistic approach is vital for developing robust and scalable embedded solutions from scratch.

My areas of specialization extend to IoT products, motor control, closed-loop systems, and RF low-power systems. I have a unique skill in developing intelligent systems that integrate advanced technologies, sleep modes, and ULP (Ultra Low Power Co-processors) to achieve optimum energy efficiency. My experience in programming these ULP co-processors using assembly language is a key aspect of my ability to create highly efficient and sustainable solutions.

My passion is centered around designing field-independent products and RF systems that serve as benchmarks in energy efficiency.
